## Get started with Fingertip + Zapier

[Zapier](https://zapier.com) lets you connect Fingertip with thousands of popular apps, automating your work without writing any code. Create powerful workflows between Fingertip and your favorite tools to save time and streamline your processes.

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Fingertip account">

    1. [Sign up for Zapier](https://zapier.com/sign-up) (it's free to start)
    2. [Log in to your Zapier dashboard](https://zapier.com/app/dashboard)
    3. Click
    ["Create Zap"](https://zapier.com/app/editor) in the top navigation
    4. Search for and select "Fingertip" as your app
    5. When prompted, enter your
    [Fingertip API key](https://fingertip.com/account/api)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a trigger">

    Select what starts your automation:
    - Choose "New Form Response" to trigger
    when someone submits a form
    - Select "New Site Contact" to trigger when a
    contact is added
    - Pick the specific form or contact type if needed

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to another app">
    Choose the destination app (like [Google
    Sheets](https://zapier.com/apps/google-sheets/integrations) or
    [HubSpot](https://zapier.com/apps/hubspot/integrations))
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up the action">
    Configure what happens when the trigger occurs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and activate">
    Run a test to verify everything works
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits of connecting Fingertip with Zapier

- **Connect to 8,000+ apps** - Integrate with tools like Google Sheets, HubSpot, Airtable and more
- **No coding required** - Build automations with a simple point-and-click interface
- **Free to get started** - Core features are free forever, with a 14-day trial for premium features

## Triggers

Events that start a Zapier workflow:

- **New Form Response**: Triggers when a new form response is created
- **New Site Contact**: Triggers when a new site contact is created
